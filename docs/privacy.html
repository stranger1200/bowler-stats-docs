Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Bowler Stats. I am committed to providing an amazing experience for viewing and sharing your Clash Royale stats while being privacy first. This policy explains what data the bot collects, why it needs it, and your rights regarding it.

Disclaimer: Bowler Stats is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Supercell. It is operated in line with the Supercell Fan Content Policy.

2. Information I Collect & Why

The bot only collects data that is strictly necessary to work for you. It does not collect personally identifiable information (PII) like your real name or email address.

Data Type Purpose Retention
Discord User ID Links your saved tags, custom settings, and achievements to your account. Until requested deletion.
Clash Royale Player Tags Allows the bot to fetch your public profile, battle logs, and chest cycle from the Supercell API. Indefinite (user-managed).
Custom Colours (Patreon) Stores your preferred hexadecimal colour code for profile customisation. For the duration of your support.
bot achievements Tracks milestones and command usage to provide progression features. Indefinite.
Discord Guild IDs Manages server-specific configurations (eg. whitelisting). Deleted when the bot leaves.
Error Logs Used for debugging and stability. Contains technical data like command names and stack traces. Stored for technical analysis.

3. How Your Data is Used

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Deliver core features, such as displaying Clash Royale stats.
  • Personalise your experience through custom colours and achievements.
  • Maintain server-specific settings.
  • Diagnose technical issues and improve bot performance.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

  • Supercell: The bot transmits your Player Tag to the official Supercell API to retrieve public game data. It never asks for, stores, or accesses your Supercell login credentials.
  • No Third-Party Sales: The bot does not sell, rent, or trade your data. Your information is never shared with advertisers or third-party brokers.
  • Legal Necessity: Data may be disclosed only if required by law or in response to a valid request by public authorities.

5. Your Rights & Data Control

You have full control over your information. You can manage your data directly through the bot:

  • View Your Data: Use /data view to see exactly what information the bot has stored associated with your Discord account.
  • Delete Your Data: Use /data delete to permanently remove your saved player tags, custom settings, and achievements from its database.

Note: Deletion is irreversible. If you use the bot again, a new, blank profile will be created.

If the bot is removed from a Discord server, all data specific to that server (Guild ID and configurations) is automatically purged from its systems.

To exercise your right to access or for any manual data enquiries that cannot be resolved via commands, please contact me via the support server.

6. Policy Changes

I may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be announced in my official Discord server. By continuing to use Bowler Stats, you automatically agree to the updated privacy policy.
